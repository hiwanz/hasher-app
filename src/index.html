About Hasher

Hasher is a powerful utility for cryptographic operations and data conversions. All calculations are performed entirely on your device — your data never leaves your computer.

Features

  • Hash: MD5, SHA-1, SHA-2 (224, 256, 384, 512), RIPEMD-160, MD4, Whirlpool
  • HMAC: MD5, SHA-1, SHA-2 (224, 256, 384, 512), RIPEMD-160, MD4
  • CRC: CRC-8, CRC-16, FCS-16, FCS/CRC-32
  • Cipher (OpenSSL compatible): AES-256, DES, Triple DES, Rabbit, RC4, RC4Drop
  • Network: Subnet calculator, IP conversions (Dec, Bin, Hex)
  • Time: Unix timestamp conversions (Datetime, RFC-1123, ISO 8601)
  • Numbers: Number system conversions (Dec ↔ Hex, Dec ↔ Bin, Dec ↔ Roman)
  • Strings: Encoding conversions (ASCII, UTF-8, UTF-16 ↔ Hex)
  • Encode: Base64, ROT-13, URI encoding, HTML encoding
  • Morse: Text to Morse code conversion

How to Use

Enter or paste your text in the input field, then click on any result to copy it to your clipboard. Some operations (like ciphering and HMAC) require a password — it will appear when needed.
